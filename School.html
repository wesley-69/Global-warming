What are Greenhouse Gases?

Click to reveal!

Greenhouse gases are gases that trap heat in the atmosphere. The primary greenhouse gases in Earth's atmosphere are water vapor, carbon dioxide, methane, nitrous oxide, and ozone.

What is the Greenhouse Effect?

Click to reveal!

The greenhouse effect is the process by which radiation from a planet's atmosphere warms the planet's surface to a temperature above what it would be without this atmosphere.

What are the Effects of Global Warming?

Click to reveal!

Global warming has a range of effects, including melting ice caps, rising sea levels, more frequent and severe weather events, and increased heatwaves.
